Features

Your governance. Your sovereignty. Our execution engine.

Velarium is a horizontally scalable execution layer that runs inside your trust boundary—whether that’s a public chain, a private network, or a fully air-gapped deployment. It’s being built to keep your governance model and sovereignty intact: no asset migration, no hosted custody, no giving up control.

Horizontal scale without monster boxes.

We’re designing Velarium as an HPC/HFT-inspired execution fabric for chains and coordination systems. The goal: scale out across commodity hardware, not up into overpriced monsters, and independently tune compute, memory, network, and storage to get predictable cost-per-transaction.

Drop-in upgrade, no rewrite.

Velarium is meant to sit under your existing chain or coordination logic. Keep your contracts, tooling, and governance as-is, while upgrading the execution layer to a parallel, high-performance fabric.

JIT contract recompilation for *your* case.

Velarium’s architecture strips away generic VM overhead. Contracts are specialized to your chain, workload, and configuration, compiled into sandboxed native code under a controlled runtime to maximize per-core performance.

Built to degrade gracefully under adversarial load.

The execution model is explicitly designed to avoid global collapse under pathological or abusive workloads. Our aim is graceful degradation with preserved aggregate throughput and protection for well-behaved traffic.

Parallelization policies & sequencer advisories.

Velarium helps your sequencer make smarter decisions. It can emit policy-based advisories to throttle or reshuffle flows within your governance rules, so pathological or abusive patterns don’t drag down everyone else—without breaking fairness.
